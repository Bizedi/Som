Here are ten beautiful girl names inspired by paradise, each with a brief meaning:

- **Sidra** – Lote tree of the highest heaven.
- **Sundus** – fine green silk mentioned in the Quran.
- **Kawthar** – abundant goodness; the river of Paradise.
- **Afnaan** – fruitful branches; a symbol of beauty.
- **Salsabeel** – a refreshing spring in Paradise.
- **Tasniim** – a blessed spring for the righteous.
- **Janaa** – garden; a reflection of Paradise.
- **Jinaan** – lush gardens that envelop with shade.
- **Lu'lu'a** – pure pearl.
- **Xuur** – radiant, bright-eyed beauty.

Choose names that reflect faith, beauty, and blessing for your daughter.
