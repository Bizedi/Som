# Somali Culture and Tradition: Preserving Culture for Future Generations

Somali culture is a beautiful culture that contains more history, customs, and values that have been shared across generations. For Somali families around the world, preserving and teaching these cultural traditions to children is important to preserve cultural identity, strengthen family ties, and ensure that future generations can access the wisdom and beauty of Somali culture.

## The beauty of Somali culture

### Historical Background
Somali culture is based on:
- **Ancient Traditions**: Traditions and customs dating back centuries
- **Oral History**: Oral stories and traditions
- **Islamic Values**: Integration of Islamic fundamentals
- **Rural Culture**: Rural cultural life
- **Marine Influence**: Maritime customs and trade

### Basic Value
Somali culture emphasizes:
- **Family Cohesion**: Strong family ties and togetherness
- **Hospitality**: Taking care of guests
- **Sacred**: Parents, elders, and society
- **Honor**: Personal and family honor
- **Community**: General responsibility and support
