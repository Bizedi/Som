** 1. Encourage children to ask questions and think critically**

* Allow the children to freely ask you the questions "why?" and "how?".
* Use examples from everyday life and encourage children to explore ideas by playing, drawing or doing simple experiments.
* Don't always give ready answers — show them the way they can find the answer themselves.

- - -

** 2. Teach emotional intelligence (Emotional Intelligence)**

* Help children name and understand their feelings (happy, sad, angry or afraid).
* Teach empathy: - ask how someone else would feel in a similar situation to them, rightly and positively.
* Be calm and respectful when resolving conflicts.

- - -

** 3. Build a child's growth mindset (Growth Mindset)**

* Praise the effort, and not just the success. Praise the child, using words such as: "You are working hard", instead of saying "you are smart".
* Teach that mistakes are part of learning in life.
* Share stories from your life where you failed and how you overcame them.

- - -

** 4. Increase Cooperation And Teamwork**

* Allow children to participate in team sports, school groups, or community hygiene.
* Encourage socializing with other children, so that the child feels a sense of belonging.
* Teach that self-help is mutual and much needed.

- - -

** 5. Encourage creativity and imagination**

* Provide resources for art, stories, and games.
* Allow them to create their own stories, songs, or pictures, even if the results are not yours.
* Celebrate innovation rather than praise for a job well done.

- - -

** 6. Connect nature to real life**

* Let the kids meet farmers, ranchers, planters, or clean up their environment.
* Experience builds responsibility, stability, and understanding of life.

- - -

** 7. Teach problem solving**

* Use simple challenges (“What should we do if it rains while we are playing?”).
* Encourage them to think of several solutions, before taking action.

- - -

**❤️ 8. Set a good example**

* Be a role model: show honesty, kindness, and discipline.
* Teach them to identify local heroes, peacemakers, and innovators.

- - -

**️ 9. Create a stable and supportive environment (Home and School)**

*Avoid yelling and physical punishment to the child, instead use calm guidance and explanations.
* Make children feel heard and trusted when they are well thought out.

- - -

** 10. Limit the influence of negative media**

* Monitor what the kids are watching and what games they are playing.
* Promote things that promote education, culture and peace.
