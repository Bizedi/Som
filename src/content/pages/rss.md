## RSS Feeds

Stay updated with the latest articles and content from Miftah Som Academy by subscribing to our RSS feeds.

### What is RSS?

RSS (Really Simple Syndication) allows you to receive automatic updates from our website directly to your RSS reader or feed aggregator. When we publish new content, you'll be notified immediately.

### Available RSS Feeds

Subscribe to our main RSS feed to receive updates on all new articles and content across all categories:

**Main Feed**: `/feed.xml` - All articles and updates

**Category-Specific Feeds**:

- Health & Nutrition: `/feed/health.xml`
- Parenting: `/feed/parenting.xml`
- Education: `/feed/education.xml`
- Baby Names: `/feed/baby-names.xml`
- Quran Studies: `/feed/quran.xml`

### How to Subscribe

1. **Choose an RSS Reader**: Select an RSS reader application or online service. Popular options include:
   - Feedly
   - Inoreader
   - NewsBlur
   - Built-in browser RSS readers

2. **Add Our Feed**: Copy the RSS feed URL and add it to your RSS reader. You can subscribe to our main feed or specific category feeds based on your interests.

3. **Stay Updated**: Your RSS reader will automatically check for new content and notify you when new articles are published.

### Benefits of RSS

- **Stay Informed**: Never miss new content from Miftah Som Academy
- **Save Time**: Access all updates in one convenient location
- **Privacy Friendly**: No need to share your email address
- **Efficient**: Get notified immediately when new content is published

### Feed Information

Our RSS feeds include:

- Links to full articles

### Technical Details

Our RSS feeds are updated in real-time as new content is published. The feeds are formatted according to RSS 2.0 standards and are compatible with all standard RSS readers.
