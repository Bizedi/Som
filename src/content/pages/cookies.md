We use cookies to improve your experience, remember preferences, and measure how visitors use our site. By continuing to browse, you agree to our cookie use.

### What are cookies?
Small text files stored on your device to help websites function. We use:
- **Essential cookies** to keep the site running (cannot be turned off).
- **Performance cookies** to understand usage and improve content.
- **Preference cookies** to remember language selection and settings.

### How you can control cookies
- You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings.
- Turning off non-essential cookies may affect some features.
- To change language without losing your choice, keep preference cookies enabled.
